Texas Resort Wouldn't Be Complete Without Massages

Enjoy the ultimate in luxurious pampering. Relieve tension and soothe nerves through a therapeutic massage by one of our experienced massage therapists. We offer a variety of massage techniques ranging from relaxing Swedish to soothing Cíbolo Stone massages. Our treatment rooms offer a tranquil space enhanced by calming music, aromatic candles, and organic massage oil.

Therapeutic massage
Our most popular massage and certainly one of our most enjoyable. Swedish massage techniques are utilized to promote relaxation and stress. 60 minutes and 90 minutes.

Deep Tissue Massage
Experience the rejuvenating effects of deep-tissue massage, from increased circulation to enhanced muscle function and elasticity. Deep tissue massage is similar to Swedish massage but is used to target areas of chronic muscle tension. 60 minutes and 90 minutes.

Couple's Instructional Massage
Each partner receives hands-on instruction in basic massage techniques and custom-blended massage oil to take home and continue the experience. 90 minutes.

Serenity Massage
Aromatherapy, hydrating hand and foot treatments and a combination of massage techniques provide serenity for all the senses and induce deep relaxation and peace. 90 minutes.

Cíbolo Stone Massage
Stiffness and soreness slowly melt away as heated stones glide along the body. Warmth from indigenous stones alleviates muscle tension and enhances the effect of this therapeutic massage. Jojoba and shea butter customized with essential oils provide a hydrating experience. 90 minutes.

In order to provide superior service to our guests, we request spa reservations for massages be made in advance.
