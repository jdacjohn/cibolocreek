overview     intimate weddings    40 guests     100 guests

For weddings up to 40 guests, the lake and the mountains in front of the hacienda provide a lovely, serene backdrop. The ceremony can be held in the open air or beneath a tent. Alternatively, the courtyard in our fort offers a rare, historic atmosphere for couples seeking a special ambience. For more adventurous groups, we could transport your guests to the scenic overlook with the ranch headquarters and the fort down below.

After the ceremony, invite your guests to join you in the fort courtyard, with a selection of hors d’oeuvres prepared by our chef and drinks served by our staff. For your wedding dinner, a seated candlelight dinner for up to 42 people in the historic Great Hall of our fort is a truly impressive occasion. Banquets in the Great Hall are memorable events and will make your wedding very special. The dinner can also be served on the lawn or under the ramada in the courtyard. Our executive chef is very creative and will prepare what you would like us to serve or offer suggestions from our gourmet cuisine. Our pastry chef can bake and decorate an innovative and beautiful wedding cake for your party. After dinner, dance into the night on our wooden dance floor under the stars or beneath a tent, with our historic fort as the backdrop.

We will obtain a singer, a guitarist, a harpist, a chamber group, or a band to provide music for your ceremony and your reception, and arrange for a justice of the peace or a clergyman to perform the ceremony. We can coordinate floral decorations on your behalf and provide babysitting services. Pickups at our private airstrip on the ranch are easily handled.

Of course, during your stay at the ranch, all of the ranch activities are available, including, with prior notice, massages and facials in our spa.

If you have other ideas about your ceremony, please call us at 432-229-3737 and let’s discuss them.
