Our primary dining room is on our porch, next to the living room, which has beautiful views of our lake and mountains. The porch also features our collection of 88 hand-carved masks from festivals all over Mexico. Our meals are served family style because most of our guests enjoy meeting new friends with whom they can have interesting conversations. For those who prefer to sit apart or for groups traveling together, we are delighted to devise individual seating arrangements.
