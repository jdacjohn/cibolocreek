For weddings of 100 guests or more, the lawn in front of the hacienda can accommodate your full party and the lake and mountains beyond will provide a beautiful, serene backdrop for the ceremony. If you would like, we can build an arbor to frame the bride and groom during the service. Afterward, invite your guests to join you in the historic fort courtyard with a selection of hors d’oeuvres prepared by our chef and drinks served by our staff.

For your wedding banquet, we will set tables up on our lawn in the open air or we can provide a tent. Our executive chef is very creative and will prepare the dinner you would like us to serve or offer suggestions from our gourmet cuisine. Our pastry chef will bake and decorate an innovative and beautiful wedding cake for your party, which will be placed in our impressive Great Hall of the fort, a memorable setting for a special cake, or a different location of your choice. After dinner, dance into the night on our wooden dance floor under the stars or beneath a tent, with our historic fort as the backdrop. We’ll be there to serve you as long as you’d like.

We will obtain a singer, a guitarist, a harpist, or a chamber group to provide music for your ceremony, help you find a band for dancing afterward, and arrange for a justice of the peace or a clergyman to perform the ceremony. We can coordinate floral decorations on your behalf and provide babysitting services. Pickups at our private airstrip on the ranch are easily handled.

Of course, during your stay at the ranch, all of the ranch activities are available, including, with prior notice, massages and facials in our spa.

If you have other ideas about your ceremony, please call us at 432-229-3737 and let’s discuss them.
