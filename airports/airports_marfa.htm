Marfa Municipal Airport (MRF) serves Marfa and Presidio County and is located about 3 nautical miles north of Marfa. The airport serves private aircraft and has an FBO. It does not have scheduled airline service.

Marfa Airport is located about 31 nautical miles by air from Cíbolo Creek Ranch and about 40 miles by road.

The FAA identifier is MRF. The airport has two asphalt runways: Runway 12/30 measures 6,214 by 75 feet and Runway 3/21 measures 5,307 by 75 feet. The latitude is 30° 22' 41.074" N (30.378076), the longitude is 104° 01' 31.790" W (-104.025497), and the elevation is 4,849 feet. The airport is served by an FBO and aviation fuel is available, 100LL and Jet-A.

The Marfa Flyer, in Marfa, Texas, 432-386-0228, offers shuttle service to Cíbolo Creek Ranch.

The only car rental in the area is Alpine Auto Rental (800-894-3463). They will deliver a car to any location in the Big Bend, including Cíbolo Creek Ranch.
