Take a look at our upcoming attractions.

The beautiful Big Bend is a vast territory with many special activities. Cíbolo Creek Ranch guests could spend weeks with us and still not be able to see it all. But, for the efficient scheduling of your time while you are with us, you might be interested in accessing event information in the area. Marfa, our county seat, is 33 miles away and the nearby city of Alpine is just 60 miles from the ranch. Fort Davis is 55 miles and Ojinaga, Chihuahua, Mexico is 27 miles from the front gate.

Some of the upcoming events at Cíbolo Creek Ranch are:

Digital Photography Seminar: Wyman Meinzer
Proudly proclaimed Texas State Photographer by the Texas State Legislator and then Governor George Bush, Wyman is renowned for his wildlife, landscape, architecture, and portrait photography. His knowledge of digital photography will be shared with a group of only 14 participants at one of the most diverse photographic locations in the United States. Wyman hosts two seminars a year at Cíbolo Creek, one in the spring and one in the fall.
