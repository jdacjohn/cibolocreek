Planning a special celebration or a memorable event to treasure for a lifetime? Cíbolo Creek Ranch delivers a remarkable level of personal attention and the luxury of an intimate resort. El Cíbolo, the largest of the three fort sites, has 21 rooms that can accommodate 44 persons, double occupancy, with 2 triple occupancy suites. Bedding configurations vary depending upon the individual room, and we have some flexibility in altering existing configurations between suites and doubles. A number of our king-size beds can be reconfigured as twin beds. And, of course, we have rollaway cots. La Ciénega has 10 rooms that can accommodate 20 people. For larger functions, particularly those involving children, rollaway bedding is available

  • Functions including family reunions, anniversary parties, and old-fashioned group holidays can be centered around a wide range of activities:
  • Formal dinners
  • Outdoor celebrations with food, music, and dancing
  • Luncheons and picnics
  • A structured stay with planned group activities, such as horseback riding, touring, and rafting on the Río Grande

For special events, the ranch can be reserved in its entirety, by individual facility, or by specific rooms. 

Our ranch staff can help you plan a family function or special event that caters to every aspect of your needs from menu selection to activities planning.
