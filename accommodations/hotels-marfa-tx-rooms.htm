Our Accommodations are a Spacious Choice for Hotels, Marfa, TX

Our spacious standard rooms have king-size, queen-size, or twin beds. All are beautifully decorated in motifs that reflect our location in Texas near Mexico. Most of the furniture is wood furniture from Mexico. The rates are $325 per night for standard rooms. We also offer a smaller room by the pool with a queen-size bed for $250 per night and a room with three twin beds, which connects to a standard room, making it ideal for families with children, for $250. The standard rooms are located in the courtyard of the main hacienda or in the lakeside hacienda. All of our rooms are very convenient to the dining and public areas. Two of our rooms have a sleeper sofa and a number of our king-size beds can be split into twin beds. All of our rooms have fireplaces to warm winter nights.

Because we are located 45 minutes from the nearest restaurants of quality, most guests choose to eat their meals here at the ranch. We offer a meal plan for three meals and hors d’oeuvres at the cocktail hour for $75 per day per person, including children over the age of 5.
