Guest accommodations are provided at:

El Cíbolo—the largest of the forts, with 21 guest rooms located in two adjoining haciendas that mirror the style of the old fort, features a well-equipped spa, swimming pool, hot tub, recreation facility, multimedia room, conference room, well-stocked lake, sporting clay shooting site, rifle and pistol range, and an extraordinary museum of historical and regional artifacts. El Cíbolo and all of its guest rooms and facilities may be reserved for up to 44 guests.

La Ciénega—located 30 minutes across the ranch from El Cíbolo, with 5 rooms in the old fort and 5 rooms in the adjoining hacienda, offers a swimming pool, hot tub, hammocks, a well-appointed kitchen, dining room, and recreation/multimedia area. La Ciénega may be reserved in its entirety for up to 20 people or by individual guest room or suite.

La Morita—the smallest, most secluded of the three forts, with an adjacent adobe guest cottage that provides a sitting room, full bath, and a separate bedroom. Due to its location—it is situated 45 minutes across the ranch from El Cíbolo—La Morita does not have electricity and it relies on oil lamps and a gas-burning water heater. Cool breezes and a gas-log stove provide air conditioning and heat. Meals are served at El Cíbolo or may be picked up at El Cíbolo on a daily basis. La Morita may be reserved for single or double occupancy by adventuresome travelers.

Due to the ranch’s remote location, it is easier to dine with us than to drive 45 minutes to a restaurant in Marfa. We offer a meal plan of breakfast, lunch, hors d’oeuvres, and dinner for $75 per guest per day, including children over the age of 5.

Our room rates do not include guest options such as guided tours, horseback riding, spa services, airport shuttles, or hunting.

At Cíbolo Creek Ranch, service is teamwork, and we provide an uncommon level of personal service and pampering for our guests—at no extra charge. We feel that gratuities are not obligatory but a personal choice. If you choose to leave a tip for our staff, please do so in our main office upon your departure, so that it can be equally distributed among all.
