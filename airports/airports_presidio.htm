Presidio Lely International Airport is located 5 nautical miles north of Presidio, Texas in Presidio County. The airport serves general aviation aircraft and does not have an FBO.

The airport is 16 nautical miles by air from Cíbolo Creek Ranch and about 21 miles by road.

The FAA Identifier is T77. The airport has one runway: Runway 17/35 is 5,200 feet long and 75 feet wide. The runway surface is asphalt and is in excellent condition. The latitude is 29° 38' 02.64" N (29.634069), the longitude is 104° 21' 41.7250" W (104.361590), and the elevation is 2,932 feet. There is no FBO at the airport, but self-service aviation fuel (100LL, Jet-A) is available with a major credit card.

The airport is an international port of entry which operates 24 hours a day, seven days a week. The port code is 2403. The port of entry authorities need to be notified in advance of arrival if you plan to clear customs and immigration at the airport.

The only car rental in the area is Alpine Auto Rental (800-894-3463). They will deliver a car to any location in the Big Bend, including Cíbolo Creek Ranch.
