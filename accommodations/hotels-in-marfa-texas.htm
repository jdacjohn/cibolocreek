Cibolo Creek Suites are a Beautiful Choice for Hotels in Marfa, Texas

We offer two beautiful suites with king-size beds and comfortable overstuffed chairs. The suites also have large, spacious bathrooms with whirlpool tubs. The Master Suite is located in the original hacienda and the larger Presidente is located in the lakeside hacienda. The Presidente has a sleeper sofa, a bar, and its own fire pit for after-dinner fires. The rate for each suite is $500 per night. Both suites have a lovely view of the lake.

Because we are located 45 minutes from the nearest restaurants of quality, most guests choose to eat their meals here at the ranch. We offer a meal plan for three meals and hors d’oeuvres at the cocktail hour for $75 per day per person, including children over the age of five.
