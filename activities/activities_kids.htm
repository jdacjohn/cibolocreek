Many children come to Cíbolo Creek Ranch and leave as happy as their parents with their visit. We are a child-friendly place, with plenty of suitable activities for kids.

For All Ages

  • Our spectacular Humvee tours, equally enjoyable for children and adults
  • Swimming in our pool
  • Enjoying the bubbling water in the hot tub
  • Paddle boating on the lake
  • Fishing for catfish, perch, and bass, with equipment provided by the ranch
  • Hiking with their parents
  • Mountain biking
  • Air rifle target shooting for children 5 years and older
  • Painting
  • Bird watching
  • Star parties observing the night sky through our 12” Meade telescope, by prior arrangement
  • Our media room has a satellite TV and is well stocked with movies and board games

For Children 12 Years and Older

  • Horseback riding for children aged 12 or older (helmets required for those under 18)
  • Rifle and pistol target shooting for children aged 12 or older
  • Sporting clay target shooting with shotguns for children aged 12 or older

For Teenagers 16 Years and Older

  • ATV tours of the ranch for those with a valid driver license who are 16 and older
