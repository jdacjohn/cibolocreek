Midland International Airport (MAF) is located 221 miles from Cíbolo Creek Ranch, about 10 miles west of Midland and 10 miles east of Odessa, accessible by Interstate 20 and SH 191.

Airlines serving Midland International Airport:

  • American Eagle
  • Continental Express
  • Southwest Airlines
  • United Express

The Marfa Flyer, in Marfa, Texas, 432-386-0228, offers shuttle service from Midland International Airport to Cíbolo Creek Ranch.

Six rental car desks are located within the airport terminal on the first level across from the baggage claim area.

The modern and bright passenger terminal is split into three levels: the lobby level accommodates ticketing and baggage claim, the upper concourse level is for boarding, and the third level houses administrative offices.
