Intimate Weddings Offer Romantic Vacation, Texas Elegance

The ranch sits in the majestic Chinati Mountains which provide a romantic and natural setting for weddings. Whether you want a simple or an elegant wedding, our historical buildings and mountain panorama provide a unique backdrop for your ceremony and the reception that will leave a lasting impression on your guests.

For very intimate weddings, our small 1800s chapel is a memorable location. For larger weddings, the 1857 fort courtyard is an unforgettable locale. For even larger weddings, we utilize the lawn with the views of the lake and the mountains beyond as the backdrop to the ceremony.

For outstanding wedding dinners, the Great Hall in our 1857 adobe fort, El Cíbolo, seats 42 people in a rare historical ambience. For the largest dinners, those up to 120 people, we arrange tables on the lawn for dinner under the stars or beneath a tent, both of which feature the 1857 adobe fort as a backdrop.

You can choose delicious meals from our gourmet ranch cuisine, which has a regional flair, or we can prepare special requests. Our executive chef is very creative and our talented pastry chef can create original wedding cakes and desserts.

After the cutting of the cake, dance into the night under the stars on our wooden dance floor to the music of your favorite band or one we help select. Our staff will be on hand to provide service until the last guest leaves.

We have 21 rooms available at El Cíbolo, the principal facility, for your guests with an additional 10 rooms for overflow at La Ciénega, another 1850s adobe fort about 30 minutes across the ranch.

Every wedding is unique. We pride ourselves on catering to your individual requests. Let our capable staff make your dreams come to life and within your budget. We are dedicated to making your wedding as carefree as possible for you and your guests.
