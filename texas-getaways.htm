Great Autumn Getaways
Andrew Harper Travel and Andrew Harper's Hideaway Report – Destinations, Fall 2004

This remarkably restored historic ranch is secluded amid 30,000 acres of dramatic scrubland plateaus, gorges, red mesas and rocky peaks near Texas’ Big Bend National Park. The enclave consists of several imposing castle-like adobe forts and Spanish-flavored dwellings housing the guest quarters; the main compound features a scenic outdoor pool and charming hacienda trimmed by an inviting lounging/dining veranda. Handstitched quilts, old photos, Saltillo tile floors and adobe corner fireplaces accent the 21 accommodations. Activities include hikes and horseback rides into the colorful mountains, 4-WD ranch tours, skeet shooting, and visits to fascinating Big Bend.
