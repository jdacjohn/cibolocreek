Spa Vacation, Texas

At Cíbolo, you are just steps away from our Fitness Center and Spa where you may continue your usual workout routine or take some time to pamper yourself with one of our professional spa services.

Treat yourself to spa services that will calm your mind, melt stress from your body, and reenergize your spirit. Relax, renew your vitality, and soothe your senses. Our talented massage therapists offer a variety of massage, facial, and body treatments designed to provide ultimate indulgence and relaxation during your stay. Organic and indigenous ingredients are featured in our selection of offerings. 

Our spa is a casual, intimate space located next to the pool, inside the Fitness Center. Enjoy a refreshing and restorative experience in the spa surrounded by elegant Southwestern decor that is enhanced by the warm glow of genuine mesquite flooring and regional artwork. The spa adjoins the swimming pool and is situated next to the lake, thus providing scenic vistas as far as the eye can see and your mind can explore.

Fitness Center
You won’t have to abandon your workout routine while visiting the ranch. Our spacious Fitness Center contains state-of-the-art equipment, including treadmills, a stair machine, a stationary bicycle, weight machines, and free weights for strengthening exercises. While working out, enjoy the satellite television or the numerous music selections. The spa’s amenities include a billiards room, an aerobics room, dressing rooms, and showers.

After a workout in the Fitness Center or a day of activity on the ranch, relax in the sauna, soak in the outdoor hot tub, or unwind in the private whirlpool for two inside the spa.

Pool
Enjoy a dip in the pool after a long, hot day in the West Texas sun. The pool is perfect for relaxing or swimming laps. After a swim, settle down with a cool beverage in one of the oversized chaises longues by the beautiful, vine-covered trellis.

Sauna
Feel your stress melt away in the cozy steam sauna, which seats up to four people comfortably. Pour water on the hot stones and let the steam work its magic.

Indoor Whirlpool
After a workout or massage, you can indulge in a relaxing bath in the oversized whirlpool tub located within the spa area.

Hot Tub
Most of us love hot tubs. Enjoy a fabulous mountain view while relaxing in our hot tub located outside the Fitness Center adjacent to the pool.
